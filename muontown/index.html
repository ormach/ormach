Free browser game
where you collect cards
to learn physics.

How it works

Each day you get resources to develop your city. Your city produces relics, that represents a physics concept or phenomenon. You use relics to complete research tasks to progress the tech tree and unlock new buildings for your city.

  • Learn as you play.
  • Manage your economy to progress faster.

Spaced repetition

IIf you are familiar with Anki cards, you may also know that spaced repetition is one of the better ways to remember things. Muon Town uses a similar method, to prevent you from loosing what you have learned.

Physics

The game has 5 sets of 10 relics that cover core physics areas. So far the game has:

  • Objects: Teaches you about object properties.
  • Gravity: Newton's laws and gravitation.
  • Planetary motion: Kepler's laws.
  • Energy: Teaches you about various energy types.
  • Electricity: Learn about voltage, electrical current and more.
